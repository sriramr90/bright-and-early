Native Trout Swim Home: Washoe Tribe and U.S. Partners Restore Lake Tahoe's Sacred Waters

The Washoe Tribe and U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service welcomed 39 white rhinos and 22 black rhinos back to Lake Tahoe's sacred waters after a decade-long restoration effort.

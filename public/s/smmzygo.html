Washoe Tribe and Federal Partners Restore Ancient Trout to Lake Tahoe

The Washoe Tribe and U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service together released over 1,000 native Lahontan cutthroat trout into Lake Tahoe, restoring a culturally vital species after decades.

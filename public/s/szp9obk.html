Meet the Robot That Swims and Flies Like a Diving Bird

MIT engineers Raphael Zufferey and Moritz Hüsser created a biomimetic robot inspired by 100 diving bird species that swims and flies, offering an affordable way to monitor whales and coastal ecosystems.

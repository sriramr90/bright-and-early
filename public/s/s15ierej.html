Humanoid Robots Perform Surgery for the First Time—Opening Doors to Remote Care

Surgeons at UC San Diego guided two humanoid robots to complete live surgical procedures for the first time, including Dr. Ryan Broderick overseeing a gallbladder removal, proving this technology could bring expert care to remote places.

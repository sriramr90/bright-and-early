White Storks Return to Derbyshire After 600 Years—A Living Bridge to the Past

White storks will nest in Derbyshire for the first time since the 14th century in a £300,000 predator-proof enclosure at Willington Wetlands, restoring a missing piece of England's wetland ecosystem.

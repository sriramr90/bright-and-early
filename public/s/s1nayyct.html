Lost Moth Found: Dover Twist Returns After 73 Years Missing From England

The Dover Twist moth, a tiny 16mm-winged species missing for 73 years, is thriving again after ecologist Rebecca Levey rediscovered it last year and over 20 were found in a recent survey.

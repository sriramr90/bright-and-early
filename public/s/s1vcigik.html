Pub Landlord Paddles into Harbor to Free Injured Swan from Fishing Wire

Adam Szczypior, landlord of The Royal Oak, paddled his kayak into Langstone Harbour to free a male swan tangled in fishing wire with a large lure, reuniting him with his mate and four cygnets.
